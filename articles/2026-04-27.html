Suspect charged with attempted assassination of Trump at Washington dinner

Source: BBC News

00:00 --:--

A California man has been charged with attempting to assassinate US President Donald Trump in an incident that has sparked a White House security review.

一名加州男子被指控企图暗杀美国总统唐纳德·特朗普，这一事件引发了白宫安全审查。

Cole Tomas Allen, 31, was also charged with two firearms offences when he appeared in court in Washington DC on Monday. He did not enter a plea.

31岁的科尔·托马斯·艾伦周一在华盛顿特区出庭时，还被指控两项枪支犯罪。他没有提出抗辩。

He was carrying a semi-automatic handgun, a pump-action shotgun and three knives as he charged past a security checkpoint at the White House Correspondents' Association dinner on Saturday, say prosecutors.

检察官称，周六在白宫记者协会晚宴上，他携带一把半自动手枪、一把泵动式霰弹枪和三把刀冲过安检点。

A Secret Service agent was hit but not seriously wounded during the attack at a hotel. The accused faces life in prison if found guilty. He is due to appear in court again on Thursday.

一名特勤局特工在酒店袭击中中弹，但伤势不重。如果罪名成立，被告将面临终身监禁。他将于周四再次出庭。

Dressed in a blue jumpsuit with a nametag for Monday's court appearance, the suspect appeared calm and was softly spoken as he answered almost every question from the judge with either "yes, your honour" or "no, your honour".

周一出庭时，嫌疑人穿着蓝色连体服，佩戴姓名标签，显得很平静，说话轻声细语，几乎对法官的每个问题都回答“是，法官大人”或“不是，法官大人”。

He stated his age and said he had a master's degree.

他报出了自己的年龄，并说自己拥有硕士学位。

Reporters in court said the defendant seemed to closely observe the proceedings, and looked directly at the judge during questions.

法庭上的记者称，被告似乎仔细观察了诉讼程序，并在提问时直视法官。

Three US marshals stood behind him, and one was at his side, while the federal prosecutor handling the case, US Attorney for Washington Jeanine Pirro, looked on.

三名美国法警站在他身后，一名在他身边，而负责此案的联邦检察官、华盛顿特区联邦检察官珍妮·皮罗则在旁听。

During Saturday night's incident, Trump, Vice-President JD Vance, cabinet members and other White House senior figures were rushed from the Washington Hilton hotel ballroom after gunfire rang out.

在周六晚的事件中，枪声响起后，特朗普、副总统JD·万斯、内阁成员和其他白宫高级人物被紧急撤离华盛顿希尔顿酒店宴会厅。

The suspect allegedly rushed through a security checkpoint one floor above the basement venue.

据称，嫌疑人冲过了地下室场地上一层楼的安检点。

"One Secret Service officer was shot in the chest, but was wearing a ballistic vest that worked," Acting Attorney General Todd Blanche told a news conference on Monday.

“一名特勤局警官胸部中弹，但他穿着防弹背心，起了作用，”代理司法部长托德·布兰奇周一的新闻发布会上说。

"This heroic officer who was hit fired five times at Allen, who was not shot but fell to the ground and was promptly arrested."

“这位英勇的警官中弹后向艾伦开了五枪，艾伦没有中弹，但倒在地上，很快被逮捕。”

Blanche said the suspect is believed to have discharged his shotgun at least once.

布兰奇说，据信嫌疑人至少开了一枪霰弹枪。

It is not clear whether the Secret Service agent was shot by the suspect, or was caught in crossfire from other law enforcement. The Secret Service agent has been released from hospital. Pirro said he had a knee injury.

目前尚不清楚特勤局特工是被嫌疑人击中，还是被其他执法人员的交叉火力击中。该特工已出院。皮罗说他膝盖受伤。

The court heard that the accused had crossed multiple state lines in order to try to kill Trump.

法庭获悉，被告跨越多个州界试图杀死特朗普。

He is said to have left his home in the Los Angeles area on 21 April, travelling by train to Chicago.

据称，他于4月21日离开洛杉矶地区的家，乘火车前往芝加哥。

On 24 April, he left Chicago, and arrived in Washington where he checked into the Hilton.

4月24日，他离开芝加哥，抵达华盛顿，入住希尔顿酒店。

The following day, he walked to the event checkpoint, and "ran through while holding a long gun", said Blanche.

第二天，他走到活动安检点，“手持长枪冲了过去”，布兰奇说。

The suspect studied at the prestigious California Institute of Technology, and worshipped at the Pasadena United Reformed Church in the Los Angeles area.

嫌疑人曾在著名的加州理工学院学习，并在洛杉矶地区的帕萨迪纳联合归正教会做礼拜。

Federal campaign finance records show he donated $25 to a Democratic Party political action committee in support of Kamala Harris for president in 2024.

联邦竞选财务记录显示，他于2024年向一个支持卡玛拉·哈里斯竞选总统的民主党政治行动委员会捐赠了25美元。

Attempting to assassinate the president carries a potential life sentence.

企图暗杀总统最高可判处终身监禁。

The other charges - transportation of a firearm between states to commit a felony and discharging a firearm in a crime of violence - both have maximum sentences of 10 years.

其他指控——跨州运输枪支以实施重罪和在暴力犯罪中开枪——最高刑期均为10年。

At Monday's news conference, Pirro said the suspect's intent, based on writing he sent to his family before the attack, was to bring down as many high-ranking cabinet members as he could.

在周一的新闻发布会上，皮罗表示，根据嫌疑人在袭击前发给家人的文字，他的意图是尽可能多地打倒高级内阁成员。

Prosecutors requested that Allen remain detained, arguing that the assassination attempt charge could be considered terrorism. Pirro said more charges could be filed.

检察官要求继续拘留艾伦，认为企图暗杀指控可被视为恐怖主义。皮罗表示可能提出更多指控。

As US investigators look into the third alleged assassination attempt against Trump, security officials are reviewing how the situation was handled, and how to beef up protection of the president.

随着美国调查人员调查针对特朗普的第三起疑似暗杀企图，安全官员正在审查事件的处理方式以及如何加强总统保护。

Many have questioned whether the security perimeter at the Washington Hilton was strong enough, why attendees were never asked to show identification at the event, and why the president, vice-president and others in the line of succession were all gathered in one place.

许多人质疑华盛顿希尔顿酒店的安全警戒线是否足够严密，为何活动从未要求与会者出示身份证明，以及为何总统、副总统和其他继任者都聚集在一个地方。

In Monday's news conference, Blanche maintained that "law enforcement did not fail" in its job to protect the event.

在周一的新闻发布会上，布兰奇坚称“执法部门在保护活动方面没有失职”。

He noted that one thing should be "clear" - the gunman was one floor away, "with hundreds of federal agents between him and the President of the United States".

他指出，有一件事应该“清楚”——枪手就在一层楼之外，“在他和美国总统之间有数百名联邦特工”。

A senior White House official told the BBC that Trump was "standing by" the leadership of the Secret Service, an arm of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) that protects high-level government officials.

一位白宫高级官员告诉BBC，特朗普“支持”特勤局的领导层。特勤局是国土安全部下属机构，负责保护高级政府官员。

White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles will convene a meeting this week to "discuss protocol and practices for major events", the official also said.

该官员还表示，白宫办公厅主任苏西·威尔斯本周将召开会议，“讨论重大活动的规程和做法”。

At a news briefing on Monday, White House Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt said Trump "believes the protocols worked" for security on Saturday and blamed rising political violence on the rhetoric of Democrats.

在周一的新闻发布会上，白宫新闻秘书卡罗琳·莱维特表示，特朗普“相信周六的安全规程发挥了作用”，并将政治暴力升级归咎于民主党的言论。

Trump has offered assurances that King Charles III will "be very safe" during his four-day state visit to the US, which began on Monday at the White House.

特朗普保证，查尔斯三世国王在为期四天的国事访问期间将“非常安全”。此次访问于周一在白宫开始。

The president's security detail must meanwhile prepare for a rescheduling of the White House correspondents' dinner.

与此同时，总统的安全细节必须为重新安排白宫记者晚宴做准备。

FBI Director Kash Patel told Fox News on Monday that "we're going to be so ready" for Trump to attend the event in the next 30 days and that "the security posture, I imagine, is going to be completely different".

FBI局长卡什·帕特尔周一告诉福克斯新闻，“我们将做好充分准备”让特朗普在30天内参加活动，并且“我想安全态势将完全不同”。

No new date has yet been set for a second try at the annual dinner.

年度晚宴的第二次尝试尚未确定新日期。

Preparations for the event typically take months.

活动的准备工作通常需要数月。

The Washington Hilton, where John Hinckley Jr attempted to assassinate then-President Ronald Reagan 45 years ago, said it followed security instructions from the Secret Service.

华盛顿希尔顿酒店——45年前小约翰·欣克利曾在此企图暗杀时任总统罗纳德·里根——表示遵循了特勤局的安全指示。

"The hotel was operating under stringent security protocols for the property as directed by the US Secret Service, which led security for the event," it said in a statement, according to Reuters, adding that it had worked with local police and other security teams.

据路透社报道，该酒店在一份声明中表示，“酒店按照美国特勤局的指示，对酒店采取了严格的安全协议”，并补充说已与当地警方和其他安全团队合作。
